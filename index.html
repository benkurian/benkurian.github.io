Student, Musician, Editor

About

I'm Ben Kurian, a 14-year-old musician with a passion for creating both music and film. I've spent a decade learning the piano, six years on the violin, four years on the drums, and two years exploring the bass guitar. Over the last three years, I've also delved into music production, which has been a journey of self-discovery and growth for me. I've participated in the National Piano Guild at a Preparatory B Level and played in the OMEA All-State Orchestra on violin. Additionally, I've been fortunate to receive recognition in global competitions like the World of 8 Billion video contest and C-SPAN's StudentCam competition. I'm dedicated to my craft and aspire to inspire others through my compositions, cinematic work, and other projects.
