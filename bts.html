About Our Presentation

Breaking the Silence: Mental Health Awareness

Our HOSA Health Education presentation focuses on destigmatizing mental health issues among adolescents and promoting early intervention strategies.

Objectives

  • Increase awareness about common mental health disorders
  • Identify warning signs of mental health struggles
  • Provide resources for seeking help
  • Promote empathy and understanding

Target Audience

  • Seventh grade classrooms of 25-30 students

Key Messages

Mental health is just as important as physical health.

It's okay to not be okay - seeking help is a sign of strength.

Early intervention can make a significant difference in outcomes.
